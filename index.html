Welcome to my implementation of Stanford CS193P Assignment 4 & 5.

This assignment date from Fall 2011 and since Apple has removed Google Maps and replaced it with their own map application. Therefore, some of the extra credits can't be done anymore. However, whenever possible, the extra credits were implemented.

The screenshots below show some of the functionalities implemented:

  • Use of Sections in the Top Places table view: The places are sorted by countries and then by cities
  • Thumbnails and accessors in the map annotations
  • thumbnails for each photo in the table view
  • iPhone and iPad screenshots
